Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2025

Quick Summary: Meow Meow is a simple cat widget app that displays cat images on your home screen. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

1. Information We Collect

We don't collect any personal information. Meow Meow is designed to work entirely on your device without requiring accounts, logins, or personal data.

What We Don't Collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Photos, files, or other personal content

2. How the App Works

Meow Meow fetches random cat images from the public CATAAS (Cat as a Service) API to display in your home screen widget. This process:

  • Downloads cat images directly to your device
  • Caches images locally for widget display
  • Does not transmit any personal information to external servers
  • Only communicates with the CATAAS API to fetch new cat images

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

CATAAS (Cat as a Service)

  • Purpose: Provides random cat images for the widget
  • Website: https://cataas.com
  • Data Shared: None (anonymous image requests only)
  • Their Privacy Policy: Available at their website

4. App Permissions

Meow Meow requests minimal permissions to function:

Internet Access

  • Purpose: Download cat images from CATAAS API
  • Usage: Only for fetching new cat images

Home Screen Widget

  • Purpose: Display cat images on your home screen
  • Usage: Show cached cat images in widget form

5. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Cached Images: Cat images are temporarily stored for widget display
  • App Preferences: Basic app settings (widget size, refresh frequency)
  • No Cloud Storage: Nothing is uploaded to external servers

6. Data Sharing

We don't share any data because we don't collect any. Since Meow Meow operates entirely on your device and doesn't collect personal information, there's nothing to share with third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Meow Meow is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's privacy.

8. Data Security

While we don't collect personal data, we still prioritize security:

  • All image downloads use secure HTTPS connections
  • Cached images are stored securely on your device
  • The app doesn't access other files or apps on your device

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Since we don't collect contact information, we recommend checking this page periodically for updates.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, etc.) don't apply. However, you can:

  • Delete the app to remove all cached images and preferences
  • Clear the app's cache through your device settings
  • Remove widgets from your home screen at any time
