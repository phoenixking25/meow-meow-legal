Meow Meow doesn't collect or store any personal account data. Since the app doesn't require accounts, logins, or personal information, there's no account data to delete from our servers.

What Data Does Meow Meow Store?

Meow Meow operates entirely on your device and only stores:

  • Cached Cat Images: Temporarily downloaded from CATAAS API for widget display
  • App Preferences: Widget settings, refresh intervals, and display options
  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device only

üì± No Server Data

Unlike many apps, Meow Meow doesn't send your data to external servers, create user profiles, or maintain account databases. Everything happens locally on your device.

How to Remove All App Data

Since all data is stored locally, you can completely remove it yourself:

Complete Data Removal:

1
Uninstall the App
Remove Meow Meow from your device through your app store or device settings. This removes all cached images, preferences, and app data.
2
Remove Widgets
Before uninstalling, remove any Meow Meow widgets from your home screen by long-pressing and selecting "Remove."
3
Clear Cache (Optional)
For extra thoroughness, go to Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Meow Meow ‚Üí Storage ‚Üí Clear Cache before uninstalling.

Alternative: Clear Data Without Uninstalling

If you want to keep the app but reset all data:

Android:

1
Go to Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Meow Meow
2
Tap "Storage" ‚Üí "Clear Data" and "Clear Cache"
3
This removes all cached images and resets preferences

iOS:

1
Go to Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí iPhone Storage
2
Find Meow Meow ‚Üí "Offload App" or delete and reinstall
3
This clears all app data while keeping the app

What Happens After Data Deletion?

  • All cached cat images are permanently removed
  • Widget preferences and settings are reset
  • Any home screen widgets will stop working
  • No trace of the app data remains on your device
  • Your privacy is completely protected

GDPR and Privacy Rights

Under GDPR and similar privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Since we don't collect data, there's nothing to access
  • Delete: Remove all local data by uninstalling the app
  • Portability: Not applicable as no personal data is collected
  • Rectification: Not applicable as no personal data is stored

üîí Privacy by Design

Meow Meow was designed with privacy in mind. By not collecting personal data in the first place, we eliminate the need for complex data deletion processes and ensure your privacy is always protected.
